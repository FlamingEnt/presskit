History

Early history

Started out making small tweaks and mods to the various id Tech powered games as well as Duke Nukem 3D and Half-Life. Began work on a beta mod for Half-Life 2, which took up a lot of senior HS year and some of my college time.

After that

After the beta mod was prretty much dead in the water, I began to heavily modify the early id Tech engines, trying to come up with a modern design all the while making something new and interesting. Decided to put something out there and see if I could build a company around that idea and here we are several years later.
